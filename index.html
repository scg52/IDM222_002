Relic

Commodore, Coriath, Cutlass, Grimoire, Ragnarok. These are the worlds of Relic. Each world has its own customs and races who each specialize in different things. Here in the Grand Archive you can read about the different worlds and the races which inhabit them. Each world is based off a different source, from the sci-fi world of Commodore to the fantasy world of Coriath, to the Eldritch world of Grimoire, there is a style for every player.
